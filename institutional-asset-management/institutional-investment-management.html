Institutional Investment Management

The key to successful investing is a consistent, disciplined process.

Institutional Asset Management > Institutional Investment Management

Your organization’s investment challenges are complex, and meeting your day-to-day and long-term financial commitments requires a financial partnership with a proven manager with deep resources. The Commerce Trust Company can provide a comprehensive program of analysis to help you and your organization understand your investment options.

We manage over $9.7 billion in assets for a wide variety of organizations just like yours. Our experienced team manages a broad range of investment strategies that are consistently applied in philosophy and approach to meet our clients’ objectives. Then we combine the historical strength of our fixed-income and equity strategies with some of the best investment products from around the world – including those otherwise accessible only to large institutional investors – to develop a custom portfolio tailored to your financial goals.

  • Separately managed accounts for fixed income or equity mandates
  • Mutual funds
  • Asset allocation – model portfolios
  • Hedging strategies for concentrated equity positions
